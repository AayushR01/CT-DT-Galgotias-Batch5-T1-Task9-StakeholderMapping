# Selected Project

## 360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services

### Project Overview

The 360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services project aims to transform traditional banking operations into a fully digital ecosystem. The initiative focuses on enhancing customer experience, improving operational efficiency, strengthening security, and enabling seamless digital transactions through modern technologies.

---

# Stakeholder Mapping

Stakeholder mapping helps identify individuals, groups, and organizations that influence or are affected by the project. Understanding stakeholder interests and influence levels ensures effective communication and project success.

---

# Primary Stakeholders

Primary stakeholders are directly involved in or significantly affected by the project.

| Stakeholder      | Role                                       | Influence        | Interest        |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ---------------- | --------------- |
| Bank Customers   | Use digital banking services               | High Impact      | High Interest   |
| Bank Management  | Strategic decision making and governance   | High Influence   | High Interest   |
| Employees        | Daily users of banking systems             | Medium Influence | High Interest   |
| IT Team          | System implementation and maintenance      | High Influence   | High Interest   |
| Software Vendors | Provide technology platforms and solutions | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |

---

# Secondary Stakeholders

Secondary stakeholders indirectly influence or are affected by the project.

| Stakeholder               | Role                                       | Influence        | Interest        |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ---------------- | --------------- |
| RBI and Regulatory Bodies | Ensure compliance and regulatory standards | High Influence   | Medium Interest |
| Government Agencies       | Regulatory oversight and governance        | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |
| Payment Gateway Providers | Enable digital payment processing          | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |
| Cybersecurity Teams       | Protect customer and banking data          | High Influence   | High Interest   |
| Investors                 | Focus on profitability and business growth | Medium Influence | Medium Interest |

---

# Power–Interest Matrix

## Manage Closely (High Power, High Interest)

These stakeholders require continuous engagement and active participation.

* Bank Management
* IT Team
* Cybersecurity Teams

---

## Keep Satisfied (High Power, Medium Interest)

These stakeholders should be informed regularly and kept satisfied.

* RBI and Regulatory Bodies
* Software Vendors
* Investors

---

## Keep Informed (Medium Power, High Interest)

These stakeholders require consistent communication and updates.

* Bank Customers
* Employees

---

## Monitor (Medium Power, Medium Interest)

These stakeholders should be monitored periodically.

* Government Agencies
* Payment Gateway Providers

# Stakeholder Responsibilities

## Bank Customers

* Use digital banking services
* Provide feedback on user experience
* Adopt new banking technologies

## Bank Management

* Define project strategy
* Allocate resources and budgets
* Monitor project performance

## Employees

* Operate digital banking systems
* Support customer interactions
* Adapt to new digital workflows

## IT Team

* Develop and maintain digital platforms
* Ensure system reliability
* Manage technical operations

## Software Vendors

* Supply software and technology solutions
* Provide technical support and updates

## RBI and Regulatory Bodies

* Monitor compliance requirements
* Establish regulatory guidelines

## Government Agencies

* Provide regulatory oversight
* Ensure legal compliance

## Payment Gateway Providers

* Facilitate secure digital transactions
* Support payment infrastructure

## Cybersecurity Teams

* Protect sensitive customer information
* Prevent cyber threats and attacks

## Investors

* Evaluate business performance
* Support long-term growth initiatives

---

# Key Insights

* Bank customers are the most important beneficiaries of digital transformation.
* IT teams and cybersecurity professionals are critical to successful implementation.
* Regulatory compliance is essential for secure and legal banking operations.
* Continuous stakeholder engagement improves project effectiveness.
* Collaboration among internal and external stakeholders ensures sustainable digital growth.

---

# Benefits of Stakeholder Mapping

* Better stakeholder engagement
* Improved communication planning
* Reduced project risks
* Enhanced decision-making
* Increased project success rate
* Stronger collaboration among stakeholders

---

# Conclusion

The success of the 360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services project depends on effective collaboration among customers, management, employees, IT teams, regulators, and technology partners. Proper stakeholder mapping helps identify priorities, manage expectations, and ensure that all participants contribute effectively to the project's goals of secure, efficient, and customer-centric digital banking services.
